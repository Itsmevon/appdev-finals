Story

Riverbend Rice Farm

Founded by our great-grandparents, who immigrated to the United States from Japan in the early 1900s, our family has been dedicated to cultivating the land and growing the highest-quality rice for our neighbors and customers.

Riverbend Rice Farm

As you wander through our fields, you'll discover a patchwork of lush green paddies, where our expert farmers carefully tend to each stalk of rice to ensure a bountiful harvest. Our commitment to sustainable farming practices means that every aspect of our operation is designed to protect and preserve the natural beauty of our land and the surrounding ecosystem.

A Story of Legacy and Tradition

Riverbend Rice Farm has been a part of the local community for generations. Founded by our great-grandparents, who emigrated from Japan to start a new life in America, our family has been farming this land for over 70 years.
